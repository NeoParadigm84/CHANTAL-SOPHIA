About Chantal Sophia our Director

Chantal Sophia is a D.C., Maryland, and Virginia based event planner but prides her business on being more than an event planner, but also an experience curator. Having created beautiful experiences for her clients ranging from baby showers, sweet sixteens, school proms, bridal showers, engagements, and of course, weddings, each event is laced in pure creativity to fit individual client needs and personalities. When Chantal began her design career in the District of Columbia more than 6 years ago, the community surrounding her recognized her creativity and uniqueness in the design process, and with the help of their urging, she officially opened Chantal Sophia Designs in 2017!

Today, Chantal and her team are known for planning some of the most unique events that are catered to her clients personalities and wishes From a reserved taste of design to an over the top design taste, there is room for you and her team to collaborate. Her clients love her genuine demeanor, innovative ideas and her ability to bring a vision to life. Whether it’s a dinner for two or a big blowout for hundreds, Chantal and her team have an eye for making their clients’ dreams come true.

Chantal Jones in her additional time, loves spending time with her family, educating the youth of South East D.C, cooking, singing, travelling , doing daily devotionals, and of course Instagramming away at @ChantalSophiaDesigns. Chantal is based in the DMV area but brings events to life wherever her team is called.
